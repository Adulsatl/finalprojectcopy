About Us

Government Polytechnic College, Nedumangad Is A Premier Institution Which Excels In The Field Of Technical Education For More Than Three Decades. It Offers Academic Programmes In Various Fields Of Engineering Such As Computer Engineering, Computer Hardware Engineering And Electronics Engineering. It Has A Team Of Vibrant Faculty And Non- Teaching Staff Dedicated To Social Commitment. Located Very Near To Nedumangad Town Which Is Remarkable For Its Historical Landmarks Like The Koyikkal Palace And Aruvikkara Dam With Its Picturesque Landscape, It Serves As A Rural College Which Caters To Impart Technical Education To Students From The Rural Areas. Established In The Year 1999, The College View To Mould Competent Engineers Who Are Responsive To Societal Needs. Government Polytechnic College, Nedumangad Strives To Establish Value Based And Need Based Education. With This Objective, The College Has Various Activities That Aims For A Holistic Development In The Students Under The Aegis Of Ncc, Nss And Sports. The Infrastructure Of The Campus Was Upgraded In 2022 With The Inauguration Of A New Academic Block And Workshop Building By The Hon. Higher Education Minister, Dr.r.bindu On 31st October,2022 With A Total Expenditure Of 7.12 Crores. The Foundation Stone For This Block Was Laid By The Former Hon. Higher Education Minister,dr.k.t.jaleel In 2018
